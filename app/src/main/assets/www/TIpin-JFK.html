In 1963, the GRCSW became a primary sponsor of President Kennedy's trip to Dallas. A proposed itinerary had him eschewing a motorcade for a visit to Richardson. It was nixed.
At the Dallas Trade Mart, Dr. Jonsson broke the news to thousands of guests. The President was slated to start his speech about national security by praising the GRCSW.
Had Kennedy been able to deliver the speech or visit the campus, it might have pushed the GRCSW further toward defense development rather than space science.
