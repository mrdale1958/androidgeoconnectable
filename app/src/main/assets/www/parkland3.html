Parklandâ€™s large number of patients led to the decision by Dallas County to create an additional building to the Parkland hospital in 2015.

The new Parkland building was designed with intentions to make it easier for visitors to find parking, minimize confusion navigating, and promote healing for patients.

Additionally, the proposers also wanted the new building to have the ER on the first floor, larger patient rooms, and better views of the city of Dallas.

Funding for the new building was made possible by donors and bond propositions, donors currently have their name engraved in glass panels in the main lobby.

The new building cost $1.27 billion dollars to make.
