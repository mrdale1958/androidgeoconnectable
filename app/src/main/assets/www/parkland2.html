Second Parkland Hospital (1952-2010)

But everything must come to an end and the first hospital was shut down...
For the second one to open up! On April 26, 1952 groundbreaking ceremonies were held for the new Parkland Hospital on 5201 Harry Hines Blvd.


Fun Fact: This hospital became the first civilian hospital in Texas to use an artificial kidney machine!

In Nov. 22, 1963, President John F. Kennedy was rushed to Parkland after he was shot by an assassin where he was pronounced dead at 1 p.m. in Trauma Room 1, despite the staff’s best efforts.

They tried hard to save the beloved President’s life

But this soon turned out to be a tragic event in American history.
